# Personas
## Persona 1:  Julian (Customer)

### Context and Role

- Role: Book a flight
- Environment: In a taxi to the Airport, alone
- Trigger: Flying home for family

### Goals and Success

#### Primary Goal

Successfully book a flight to home.

- Able to get valid boarding pass
- Able to validate identiy 
- Able to change date or refund 

### Pain Points

- No seats on plane 
- Cannot book close to departure 
- Payment Doesnt go through (re-enter details)
- Identification checks fail
- Needs to print a boarding pass
- Complain if website is slow/confusing 
- Give up when no plane to home airport

#### Workarounds:

- Use another airline
- Different Cards
- Go to help desk (plead)
- Later departure 

### Constraints and Non-Goals

#### Constraints

- fixed destanation airport 
- stressed (close to deadline)
    - worried about baggage rules
- mobile user 
- low connection (travelling on motorway)

#### Non-Goals

- doesn't care about seat
- doesn't care about in-flight food/amenities
- price insensitive 
- doesn't care about which airline

## Persona 2:  Izabelle (baggage handler)

### Context and Role

- Role:  Manage baggage + validate incoming customers
- Environment: Land-Side of airport
- Trigger:  Get through workday without complaints

### Goals and Success

#### Primary Goal

Ensure the ticket is valid and ticket holder matches

### Pain Points
- Slow system
- loud environment 
- large amounts of luggage
- unprepared / annoying customers 

#### Workarounds:
- delegate task to other employees
- enter buttons by rote rather than correctly
- call airport police

### Constraints and Non-Goals

#### Constraints
- deal with inflow of customers (as flight approaches)
-  time
- lots of task to do (identity, baggage, validate ticket)

#### Non-Goals
- type of luggage
- flight destination
- group size 
- identity of customer (excl. pregnant)

## Persona 3:  Ilario (Customer)

### Context and Role

- Role: Plan a holiday on budget
- Environment: Home, with kids (during Christmas Break)
- Trigger: Easter School Holidays

### Goals and Success

#### Primary Goal

Book a flight for the entire family  (2parents+2kids (10yr, 8yr)

- Get seats next to each other (kids sit next to parent(s))
- Window seats for Kids
- Cheapest Seats in Economy (on a budget)
- view/book flights in future
- No pressure on speed of form entry

### Pain Points
- kids (loud)
- passport validity 
- hasn't flown with kids before (unsure of process)
- don't know if kids with eat airline food
#### Workarounds:
- can call airline if need to sort details
- has time to renew passports / research
- can use airline FAQs to get more info about process

### Constraints and Non-Goals

#### Constraints
- unfamiliar airport 
- unfamiliar airline
- price
- luggage maximums 
- length of stay 
- return tickets 
#### Non-Goals
- location
- precise destination 
- airline
- time of day it departs 
- flight length

## Persona 4:  Donovan (System Admin)

### Context and Role

- Role: Manage bookings and flight cancellations 
- Environment: Airline Office
- Trigger: Stressed as serval flights have been delayed

### Goals and Success

#### Primary Goal

get as many customers to destinations as possible

#### Non-Goals
